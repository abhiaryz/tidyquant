About Us

Headquarted in West Yorkshire, UK, Tidyquant is an AI start-up company that has been continuously developing niche' solutions in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Machine and Deep learning.
We also take pride in our expertise in IoT (Internet of Things) devices. By bridging the gap between artificial intelligence and embedded electronics, we have been developing prototypes for smart IoT devices such as Tidybox, Tidymatics and Tidycare.

At Tidyquant, we are a team of seasoned data scientists, engineers, digital marketers and business analysts who are highly passionate and committed to solve real world business challenges. Our team work across the entire end-to-end product development cycle starting from gathering business requirements, ingesting the data locked away in silos, turning them into scalable coding engines, data analysis and producing world-class BI reports to unearth valuable insights.
